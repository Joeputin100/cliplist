# Privacy Policy — My Playlist Creator 2026

_Last updated: 2026-05-31_

**My Playlist Creator 2026** ("the app") creates `.m3u` playlists for SanDisk Clip Sport
players. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with
your data.

## Your files stay on your device

You grant the app access to a music folder using Android's **Storage Access Framework**
(SAF) — the standard system folder picker. The app reads file and folder names to build
playlists, and, if you turn on the cleaning options, renames the files and folders you
choose. **All of this happens entirely on your device.** Your music, your file and folder
names, and the playlists the app creates are **never uploaded, copied off your device, or
sent to us or to anyone else.**

The app requests only access to the specific folder you pick (no broad "all files"
permission), and you can revoke that access at any time in Android's settings.

## What the app collects

- **Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics).** If the app crashes, a diagnostic report —
  device model, Android version, app version, and the technical stack trace — is sent to
  Google Firebase Crashlytics so we can find and fix the bug. These reports **do not
  contain your music, your file names, or your folder contents.** Google's handling of
  this data is described at <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.
- **Nothing else.** The app has **no account or login**, collects **no personal
  information**, uses **no advertising identifier**, shows **no ads**, and does **not sell
  or share** any data with anyone.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from
children.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app introduces a new use of data — for example, lightweight,
non-interruptive ads — this policy will be updated and the change described here **before**
that version is released.
